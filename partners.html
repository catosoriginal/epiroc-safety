Partnerships

Epiroc collaborates with global industrial, technology, and research organizations to develop safer and more advanced mining solutions.

Mining Companies (Customers & Technology Collaboration)

  • Newcrest (gold mining and extraction)
  • Hindustan Zinc (major zinc mining operations in India)
  • Ivanhoe Mines (advanced mining projects with electrification focus)
  • Boliden (European mining and metals company)
  • KGHM (copper and metal mining operations)

These partnerships support the implementation of automation, safety systems, and electrified mining equipment

Technology Partners (Industrial & Digital Alliances)

  • Ericsson (5G connectivity for mining operations)
  • Mobilaris (underground positioning and navigation systems)
  • AFRY (engineering and industrial digitalization)

These collaborations enable digital mines and remote-controlled operations

Sustainability & Materials Partners

  • SSAB (fossil-free steel for mining equipment)
  • LKAB (iron ore supply chain collaboration)
  • Vattenfall (renewable energy for production processes)

Focused on CO₂ reduction and green mining initiatives

Academic & Research Partners

  • Luleå University of Technology
  • RWTH Aachen University
  • Various EU-funded mining research projects

Focused on future automation, AI development, and mining innovation

Ecosystem Impact

These collaborations support continuous innovation in safety, automation, and sustainable mining technologies across the industry.
