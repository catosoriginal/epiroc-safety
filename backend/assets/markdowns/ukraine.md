Epiroc supports industrial development in Ukraine by providing equipment, engineering solutions, and technical services for mining and construction sectors.

The Ukrainian industrial environment requires high adaptability due to geological complexity, operational intensity, and infrastructure conditions. Epiroc provides solutions tailored to these challenges while maintaining global quality standards.

Support includes equipment supply, technical maintenance, operator training, and system integration services. These services ensure that industrial operations remain efficient and reliable throughout their lifecycle.

The company also focuses on transferring global engineering knowledge to local teams, improving operational competence and technological capability in the region.
