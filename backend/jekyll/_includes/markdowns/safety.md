Safety is a core principle within Epiroc and is integrated into all aspects of industrial operations. The company operates in high-risk environments such as mining, drilling, and heavy construction, where strict safety control is essential.

Safety is not treated as a separate function but as a system of engineering, behavior, and operational discipline. Every process is designed to minimize risk exposure and ensure controlled execution of industrial activities.

The company follows the Zero Harm principle, aiming to eliminate injuries, accidents, and hazardous incidents. This includes both physical safety and the prevention of operational failures.

Safety systems include engineering protections, operational procedures, training programs, and digital monitoring tools. Employees are trained to identify risks, follow procedures, and take responsibility for safe execution of tasks.

A key principle is Stop Work Authority, which allows any employee to halt operations if a safety risk is identified. This ensures that safety always takes priority over production.
