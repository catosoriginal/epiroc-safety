Epiroc offers a wide range of industrial equipment designed for mining, construction, and infrastructure applications. The product portfolio includes drilling rigs, underground mining machines, surface excavation equipment, rock reinforcement systems, and specialized tools for precision industrial operations.

All equipment is engineered for high-load environments where mechanical stress, vibration, temperature variation, and continuous operation are standard conditions. Reliability and durability are essential design principles for every product.

Drilling systems are developed for both surface and underground applications, offering precision control, stability, and efficiency in rock excavation processes. Underground machinery is designed to operate in confined and hazardous environments where maneuverability and robustness are critical.

Surface equipment focuses on large-scale excavation and material handling, ensuring high productivity and operational consistency. Each system is supported by service infrastructure and maintenance programs that ensure long-term operational reliability.

The company also integrates digital monitoring capabilities into its equipment, allowing operators to track performance, detect anomalies, and optimize usage in real time.
