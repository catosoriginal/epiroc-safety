About Epiroc

Epiroc is a global leader in mining and infrastructure equipment, delivering advanced solutions for drilling, excavation, and underground operations worldwide. The company operates in highly demanding environments where reliability, precision, and efficiency are critical. At its core, Epiroc is more than an equipment manufacturer — it is a safety-first engineering organization. Every machine and system is designed with a strong focus on protecting operators, preventing accidents, and reducing risks in complex industrial conditions.

Safety is No Accident. It is Technology.

A new generation's perspective on safety standards in extreme environments with Epiroc.

For a long time in industry, it was believed that basic protective equipment — helmets, protective clothing, and tools — was sufficient to ensure workers’ safety. However, modern working conditions in the mining and construction industries require a significantly more comprehensive approach. The company Epiroc has changed this perception, taking safety to a fundamentally new level.
